Elon Musk's Early Life and Education

Elon Musk’s mother, Maye Musk, is a Canadian Model and the oldest woman to star in a Covergirl campaign. Musk’s father, Errol Musk, is a wealthy South African engineer. Elon spent his early childhood in South Africa. Elon’s parents later got divorced when he was 10 years old. At 17 years old, Elon moved to Canada to attend Queen’s University to avoid mandatory service in the South African military. In 1992, Elon left Canada to study business and physics at the University of Pennsylvania. Later, Elon graduated with an undergraduate degree in economics. In 1995, Elon headed to Stanford University with the hope of getting his Ph.D. However, Two days after being part of Stanford, Elon dropped out because of the Internet Boom.
