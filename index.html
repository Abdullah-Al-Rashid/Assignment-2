Who is Elon Musk?

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and businessman who found X.com known today as PayPal), SpaceX, and Tesla Motors. Elon Musk became a multimillionaire in his late 20s after he sold his startup company, Zip2, to a division of Compaq Computers. In addition, Elon Musk then became a billionaire after eBay acquired PayPal for $1.5 billion. Elon Musk made headlines in May of 2012 when SpaceX launched a rocket that would send the first commercial vehicle to the ISS.
